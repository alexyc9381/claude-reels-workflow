# CREATOR MATRIX — the cross-creator fusion layer
> Synthesizes the 5 Script DNA docs (`creators/*-dna.md`) + the 5 topic docs (`topic-ideas/*.md`) against Alex's `SHARED-CONTEXT.md` HARD RULES. This is the router + fusion protocol: turn "topic X + creator Y" into a finished Alex reel, and know which Y to reach for. Fetched/built 2026-07-12.

The five voices are surface variations on ONE skeleton (see THE UNIVERSAL SPINE). Pick a creator for the *flavor* the topic wants; the spine and Alex's HARD RULES stay constant underneath.

## 2. The 5 voiceprints (side by side)

- **raycfu** — A calm insider leaning across the table to hand you one secret: "Most people don't realize [number]… here's how," then 3-5 crisply named steps each with a why and a dollar figure, prompts gated behind one word.
- **mavgpt** — A hyped tour guide who opens on a scary-or-magic yes/no question, walks you click-by-click through a live screen in short imperatives ("open Claude and say… now here's the trick"), spiking every 8-10s with a 4-word shock beat, prompts gated.
- **nateherk** — A fast, confident builder-to-builder show-and-tell that opens on the finished result, narrates the screen in real time with "boom" reactions, stacks real numbers and named companies, gates the full method.
- **nicksaraev** — A mildly-annoyed ex-agency guy who news-pegs the drop, calls the hype BS, deflates the complexity ("it's literally just a prompt"), drops hard numbers as receipts, then dares you to go cash in.
- **cindiezhu** — A warm "let me put you on" friend who opens "Did you know Claude can now ___?", kills your objection before you raise it ("before you spend thousands, try this"), and re-translates every feature into a plain outcome with "which means."

## 3. THE UNIVERSAL SPINE (the shared skeleton under the surface)

All five, stripped to the bone, run the **same six beats**. The names differ; the functions are identical. This is the reusable template every reel inherits before a creator flavor is layered on.

| Beat | Universal function | raycfu | mavgpt | nateherk | nicksaraev | cindiezhu |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| **1. GAP HOOK** | Open a curiosity loop; land the value noun early with a number | "Most people don't realize [n]…" | "What happens when you ask Claude to…?" | "Here's how to [outcome]…" | "[X] just dropped…" | "Did you know Claude can now…?" |
| **2. FRAME FLIP** | Reframe/promise/undercut so the body feels earned | "This is how, in N steps" | first-person receipt (→3rd person) | "This isn't [assumption], it's…" | "everyone overcomplicates it" | "before you spend $X, try this" |
| **3. NAMED MECHANISM ×N** | Break the system into 3-5 nameable chunks, each = plain what + a why/outcome | "The Surgeon." | "So first… Now…" prompts | enabler name-drop + walkthrough | "it's three agents: a CFO, a…" | "Number one, the recruiter… which means…" |
| **4. RETENTION SPIKE** | One re-hook right before the payoff beat | "this is the most important part" | "now here's the trick" | "boom" reaction / anaphora reveal | "the wild part" | "OK so this is the fun part" |
| **5. RECEIPT** | Third-person proof + a hard number (Alex-safe) | "a team runs 20 agents → $10M" | "people are finding hundreds of $$" | "a coach cleared 200 emails" | "$8K → $2, 1.5% → 2.7%" | "agencies make 100s of ads while they sleep" |
| **6. GATED CTA** | Convert curiosity to a comment; withhold the exact method | "Comment [word]" | "follow + comment [TITLE]" | "Comment [KEYWORD] and it's yours" | "Want the exact prompt? Comment [word]" | "Comment [word]" |

**The spine as a fill-in template:**

```
[GAP HOOK: novelty/secret/result/news/question that lands VALUE NOUN by word ~12, with a number]
[FRAME FLIP: promise the steps / undercut the hard way / preempt the objection]
[MECHANISM 1: name it → what it does in plain words → why that matters / the outcome]
[MECHANISM 2: name it → what → outcome]
[MECHANISM 3: name it → what → outcome]
[RETENTION SPIKE: "the most important part / here's the trick / the wild part" → the differentiator]
[RECEIPT: a third-person role runs this and gets a specific number]
[GATED CTA: Comment [ONE-WORD KEYWORD].  ← hard stop, no tail]
```

**What's universal across all five (the invariants):**
1. Value noun by ~word 12, a **number lives in the hook**, no throat-clearing (first word is the hook).
2. The body is **named chunks**, not prose — steps/personas/agents/prompts you can picture.
3. **High number density** — a figure every few sentences; specific beats vague.
4. **Third-person receipts**, never a first-person flex (this is what makes all five Alex-compatible once de-I'd).
5. **Gate the how** — show the architecture + outcome, withhold the exact prompt/file/config.
6. **One-word keyword CTA**, cut hard.

## 4. FUSION PROTOCOL (topic X + creator Y → finished Alex reel)

A literally-followable algorithm. Run top to bottom.

**Step 0 — Inputs.** You have `[TOPIC X]` (from a topic-ideas doc or the Outlier Engine) and, if unspecified, run the CREATOR ROUTER (§5) to pick `[CREATOR Y]`.

**Step 1 — Load creator DNA.** Open `creators/<Y>-dna.md`. Pull §1 (opener template + variant slot), §2 (beat map), §3 (per-beat micro-templates), §5 (phrase bank), §11 (ready-to-run template).

**Step 2 — Fill the §11 template.** Fire that creator's §11 skeleton with TOPIC X in every slot. Use their §3 micro-templates for each beat and salt in 2-4 verbatim phrases from their §5 bank so it *sounds* like Y (raycfu's "the most important part"; mavgpt's "every single"; nate's "boom" + one anaphora reveal; nick's "here's the thing everyone overcomplicates" + rule of three; cindie's "which means"). This produces a raw draft in Y's native voice — including Y's rule-breaking habits.

**Step 3 — Apply Alex's HARD RULES as explicit transforms.** Run the raw draft through these, in order. Each is a find-and-fix, not a vibe:

1. **Strip em-dashes.** Replace every `—` with a comma, period, or colon. (Rule 1. Most creators' ASR has none; cindie's cadence risks them.)
2. **De-first-person → third-person receipt.** Find every "I tried this / I built / I used to / how I set this up." Rewrite:
   - anecdote loop-closer (mavgpt's "I tried this and it…") → `People are running this and [same visceral result]` — keep the affect word ("actually/literally"), kill the "I."
   - credential flex (nick's "I used to sell websites for a living") → the general truth: `This used to cost $10K a pop.`
   - capability flex (nate's "I built these") → `Someone just built four of these in 15 minutes` OR a role receipt: `a coach ran this and…`
   - (Rule 2 + Rule 6: the receipt must be true/general, never an invented specific run or a quote in a named person's mouth.)
3. **Gate the how.** Find any place the VO hands over a complete, replicable method (mavgpt/raycfu speak full prompts; nick gives the repo away free). Replace the word-for-word method with the *shape* + the artifact name, and move the exact text behind the keyword: `The full prompt is the part that actually makes this work.` Exception: rough partial spoken prompts in a follow-along ARE allowed as value; the full set is still gated. (Rule 3.)
4. **Cut on the keyword.** Delete every tail after the CTA keyword — raycfu's "follow for more advice…", mavgpt's "and I'll shoot them over to you", nate's "click the play button / thanks guys", cindie's "and I'll send it over", nick's "catch y'all next time." Last word of the script = the keyword. (Rule 4.)
5. **De-jargon (12-year-old parse) + value noun by ~word 12.** Swap any term a 12-year-old wouldn't get: "MCP/connector/webhook" → "one connection you set up once"; "orchestrator" → "the boss agent"; "out-of-sample gate" → "a test on data it's never seen"; "ICIR/validation loss" → "a consistency score." Confirm the value noun (the money/hours/customers outcome) lands by word ~12. (Rule 5.)
6. **Length.** Trim to `words ≤ target_seconds × 4.3` (35-45s ≈ 150-190 words; listicles may run 55-70s). (Rule 5.)
7. **Breadth litmus.** Read it as a freelance designer, a Shopify owner, a coach, AND a founder. If only a technical founder can act, broaden the topic or the receipt. (Rule 7.)

**Step 4 — Self-check against the Script Factory gates.** Do not skip; these are the same bars Stage 1/3/4 enforce (§6 maps the handoff):
- **Stage-1 kill-gate (topic sanity):** value noun is money/time/or a screenshot-able deliverable; audience-size score ≥8 on the *harsh* qualifier reading; payoff is a visually-legible artifact changing state in <2s sound-off (cerebral-payoff test); opening lever isn't the same family as the last 2 in `memory/reel-lever-ledger.md`. Any FAIL → fix the topic before writing more.
- **Hook gate (`memory/claude-reel-hook-library.md`, all 10 checks):** drafted 10+ first lines, scored, picked top 2-3 across ≥2 hook families; punch in sentence ONE; mute-readable number/contrast on frame 1; specificity baits the STAKE not the tool; a verified family (not a KILLED pattern — no identity-callout, no "nobody's talking about this," no product-showcase); every curiosity/question loop is paid off later; sounds human. If top two hooks are within 1 point, present both to Alex.
- **Stage-3 draft constraints:** re-confirm ≤ target×4.3 words, zero em-dashes, but/so causality, gate-the-how held, any surviving first-person claim flagged TRUTH-REQUIRED (or reworded out now).

**Step 5 — Output.** Deliver the finished ~35-45s script + the keyword + a one-line note of which creator flavor and hook variant fired. This is a Stage-3 draft ready to enter the Stage-4 adversarial gate.

**One-paragraph version:** pick creator via router → open `creators/<Y>-dna.md` → fire its §11 template on the topic with 2-4 phrases from §5 → run the 7 HARD-RULE transforms (strip em-dashes, de-I to third-person receipt, gate the how, cut on keyword, de-jargon + value-noun-by-12, length, breadth) → self-check against Stage-1 kill-gate + the 10-point hook gate + Stage-3 constraints → ship the draft to Stage 4.

## 5. CREATOR ROUTER (topic shape → whose DNA to borrow)

Read the topic's *shape*, then borrow that creator's DNA. The spine (§3) is constant; you're choosing the flavor that fits the topic's natural energy.

**Quick decision list (first match wins):**
1. Topic is **"point Claude at YOUR data and watch it expose + fix something," doable tonight, prompt-shaped** → **mavgpt** (Q-Hook follow-along; info→artifact→agent→loop ladder). *e.g. "find every subscription draining your account."*
2. Topic is a **named multi-agent money system / a build with 3-5 nameable roles / "here's the machine"** → **raycfu** (insider-gap + named-step grammar). *e.g. "the 3-agent setup that runs your inbox."*
3. Topic is a **frontier capability, a new skill/tool, or a "just dropped" news moment with a finished artifact to show** → **nateherk** (result-first or news-drop + enabler name-drop + screen walkthrough). *e.g. "the connector that plugs Claude into everything you own."*
4. Topic is a **$-result / receipts build, an overnight optimizer loop, or "this kills your $/mo tool"** → **nicksaraev** (news-peg + skeptic-deflate + hard-number receipts + scale-out). *e.g. "the cold email that improves itself while you sleep."*
5. Topic is a **persona/outcome for the broadest, least-technical audience — "turn Claude into a named specialist/team," mute-readable, zero-jargon** → **cindiezhu** (novelty question + objection-preempt + persona listicle + "which means"). *e.g. "the 5 agents that run a founder's marketing team."*

**Tie-breakers / overlaps:**
- **Multi-agent teams** qualify for raycfu (name the machine), cindie (persona listicle), OR mavgpt/D (spin up the team as the "trick" beat). Pick by audience: raycfu = builder who wants the architecture; cindie = broad/non-technical; mavgpt = doable-tonight follow-along.
- **Overnight / autonomous** fits mavgpt (the escalation ladder tops out here), nick (the autoresearch loop), nate (Fable "one prompt, walked away"), cindie (Sub-lane C). Pick by whether the payoff is a **loop with a metric** (nick), a **live screen artifact** (nate), a **doable follow-along** (mavgpt), or a **named team** (cindie).
- **News-peg / "just dropped"** → nate (react + show the artifact) or nick (react + deflate the hype). Choose nick when the honest move is skepticism; nate when the move is awe at a real result. **Both carry a hard freshness gate** — only ship while the drop is genuinely current.
- **Contrarian / "your AI is lying to you" / "the hyped thing is theater"** → nick (Lane D, evidenced skeptic-deflate) or cindie (Sub-lane D, "stop the yes-man" behavior fix). nick for a public wave; cindie for a personal behavior config.
- **Skills / slash-command roundups** → cindie (persona listicle) or raycfu (category/numbered grammar). cindie for breadth; raycfu when each skill needs a "why it matters."

**Router one-liner:** *spoken-prompt consumer follow-along → mavgpt · named-module money system → raycfu · frontier capability / news-react → nateherk · receipts / $-result build → nicksaraev · persona + no-jargon outcome → cindiezhu.*
