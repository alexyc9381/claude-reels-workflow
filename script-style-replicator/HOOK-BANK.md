## 0. How to use

This is a swipe file, not a copy file. Every line below already beat the scroll on a real account, so it is a proven *shape* — pull the shape, refit the [SLOTS] to your Claude/AI-for-founders topic, then run the rewrite through the 10-point HOOK GATE (`claude-reel-hook-library.md`) before you record. Higher view count = stronger signal that the shape travels.

**The normalization caveat (read before trusting a single number):** the raw view counts are **NOT normalized across creators**. Each creator has a different baseline audience, so a headline number only means something *relative to that creator's own median* (Section 4 gives both). cindiezhu's corpus median is ~6.7K, so her 292K "Did you know…" outlier is a ~44x jump over her own baseline — a far bigger *relative* signal than mavgpt's 735K, which is only ~2x his 349K median. rileybrown's 1M is his single biggest but only ~5.5x his 183K median. Read the *lift over that creator's median*, not just the raw digit. When in doubt, trust a shape that outperformed for its own creator AND appears across multiple creators. (Second caveat, minor: ASR mangles proper nouns — "Cloud code," "Enthropic," "Quad Opus," "chat TPT," "PodCode," "Open Claw." These are silently normalized to Claude Code, Anthropic, Claude Opus, ChatGPT, Claude Code, OpenClaw. Wording is otherwise verbatim.)

## 5. ⛔ Do NOT use (kill-list cross-check)

Patterns that tested **dead** in verification (`claude-reel-hook-library.md`) — never build a hook on these:

- **Identity-callout: "If you're a [founder/freelancer], watch this."** (scored 0–3.)
- **First-person investigator: "I went deep and found something nobody's talking about."** (0–3.)
- **Pure product/outcome showcase as the whole hook** — the claim that "showcase = highest performer" was falsified (0–3). Showcases still appear, but a bare "look what I made" without a number/promise/awe-reveal attached does not carry.
- **Fabricated retention stats: "X% watch past 3 seconds."** (most are invented.)
- **Guardrail (Alex's audience): scammy income/follower flexes** — "$43→$2,109," "0 to 50k followers." Reads get-rich-quick to a founder audience; use domain-relevant proof shown on screen instead.

**Cross-check of the 65 corpus hooks:**

- ⚠️ **Identity-callout — one real brush.** sabrinaramonov's "If you're a business owner struggling to use AI to save time, make more money, and build leverage…" (14K, Curiosity #18) is the closest thing in the corpus to the killed "If you're a [role], watch this." It *survives* only because it self-selects by a specific SYMPTOM ("struggling to save time / make money") and immediately offers empathy + a path, not a bare "watch this." For Alex: usable **only** as symptom-self-select ("if your inbox already scares you at 7am…"), never as a flat role-callout. Keep the symptom, drop the "if you're a business owner."
