# nicksaraev — Topic Ideas (for Alex's faceless Claude reels)

> **These are HYPOTHESES.** Each still passes Script Factory Stage-1 kill-gate + a freshness check before scripting. Nothing here is greenlit; the point is to give the writer 14 pre-vetted swings that sit in nicksaraev's proven lanes, fit Alex's Claude-for-founders niche, and survive transfer to a clay-mascot reel.

Source of truth: `creators/nicksaraev-dna.md` (§11 skeleton + §1 opener variants) and the 7 top-outlier transcripts (162K–314K views).

## The engine every idea rides
Nick's transferable machine = **big fresh thing everyone is overcomplicating → here's how simple it actually is → hard-number receipt → now scale it → gated CTA.** Freshness is the click ("just dropped"), skepticism buys trust, complexity-collapse pays off, specific numbers read as honesty.

## The 4 proven sub-lanes (from the outliers)
- **Lane A — "Design/build just got commoditized in minutes."** (Nano Banana/Kling 314K, Gemini/Antigravity 254K) — one prompt → a $5–10K-looking asset for a couple dollars.
- **Lane B — "Claude just shipped X and it kills your $/mo tool."** (Routines 210K, Managed Agents 176K) — a feature drop replaces an automation stack / a hire.
- **Lane C — "Autoresearch: an overnight loop that optimizes a money metric."** (Karpathy Autoresearch 168K, Skills Autoresearch 162K) — metric + a thing to change + a loop → it self-improves while you sleep.
- **Lane D — "The hyped thing is mostly theater; here's what actually makes money."** (Clawdbot Sucks 194K) — skeptic-deflate the wave, reframe to the version that prints.

## §11 template + opener-variant legend
Every idea fires the **§11 skeleton** (DROP → DEFLATE → RECEIPT → PROOF → SCALE-OUT → gated CTA). What changes is the **opener variant** (§1):
- **[Primary Drop]** — "`[Claude / a free repo / someone]` just `[shipped/dropped/built]` `[VALUE NOUN]`."
- **[Variant A · Skeptic-deflate]** — "Everybody's hyping `[THING]`. `[Here's the truth]`."
- **[Variant B · Confession-tension]** — "You love `[THING]`. But `[the flaw this fixes]`."

## KILL-LIST guard (read before scripting any of these)
Banned hooks from the library: (1) identity-callout "If you're a `[identity]`, watch this"; (2) "I went deep and found something nobody's talking about"; (3) generic product/outcome showcase with no peg or tension. **Lane D lives one inch from #2** — the fix is that Nick's skeptic-deflate names the *specific hyped thing everyone already sees* and calls it out, it never claims secret private discovery. Ideas carrying that risk are flagged **⚠︎KILL-WATCH** below.
