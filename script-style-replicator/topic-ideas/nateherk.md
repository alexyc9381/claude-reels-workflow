## KILL-LIST flags (hook-library risks to watch at script time)
The batch was written to dodge the three banned patterns, but three ideas sit near an edge and must be steered at scripting:

The §11 ENABLER NAME-DROP beat is the fix — never skip it.
- **None** of the 15 use "I went deep and found something nobody's talking about." If a draft drifts toward it during scripting, cut it.
