# KILL-LIST — angles that flopped or are banned (check BEFORE any topic enters)

Read at `GENERATE.md` Step 0. If a topic matches anything here, it does NOT enter without a
fundamentally different reframe that beats the reason it failed. Cross-references
`memory/claude-reel-hook-library.md` (killed hook patterns) and `memory/fable-idea-backlog.md`
(killed premises + Alex's running kill record).

## Flopped in the wild (Alex's own results — do not resurface)
- **BILLS / "ask Claude to lower every bill you're overpaying on" + the whole personal money-audit
  family** (lower-your-bills, negotiate-your-bills, subscription-audit, "find money leaking from your
  accounts") — **did NOT work in the past (Alex, 2026-07-12).** The money-audit consumer angle is dead
  until a genuinely different mechanism/proof beats whatever made it fall flat. Don't pitch it again.

## TAM kills (too small an audience)
- Topics that require **running a business with clients / invoices / a team** (a "finance team," an
  agency, a CRM, a sales pipeline) — the TAM is business-owners only (Alex, 2026-07-12). Prefer topics
  **any individual can use**, business or not. Litmus: could a student, an employee, a parent, a
  freelancer, AND a founder all use it? If it needs "your clients" or "your revenue," the TAM is small.

## Structural hook kills (from the hook library)
- identity-callout "If you [identity], watch this"
- "I went deep and found something nobody's talking about" investigator tease
- generic product/outcome showcase ("this tool is amazing")
- most fabricated "X% watch past 3s" stats
- (full list: `memory/claude-reel-hook-library.md`)

## How to use
Before scripting: grep the topic against this file + the hook-library killed patterns. A near-match
must either be dropped or reframed with a receipts-first angle that names WHY the new version beats the
dead one. Log new flops here after the post-autopsy (48-72h) so the generator keeps learning.
