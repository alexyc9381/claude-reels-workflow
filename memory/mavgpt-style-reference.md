**SCRIPT FORMULA (verbatim beats from the 440K "Identity" + 716K "Photo" winners):**
1. Q-hook: "What happens when you ask [AI] to [concrete scary/valuable thing, named in full]?" — his outliers are overwhelmingly this shape; baseline videos that skip it underperform.
2. First-person proof: "I tried this and it actually [visceral result] while I slept / I didn't even know existed." (⚠️ Alex adaptation: no-anecdote rule → replace with tension line or third-person receipt; keep the word "actually" — it's the affect carrier.)
3. Follow-along imperative, his signature cadence: "So first, you want to open [app] and say [FULL PROMPT SPOKEN]."
4. Shocked reaction beat: "The results were actually terrifying." (load-bearing retention spike after each prompt)
5. Continuity cue: "Now you just want to stay in the same chat and say..."
6. Escalation flag: "Now here's the trick." + a real UI click-path spoken step by step (customize → connectors → install Chrome / hit the plus, turn on agent mode)
7. Delegation payoff: "...while you just sit there."
8. "And for the bonus prompt..." + recurring-loop close ("recheck every week until I'm completely gone")
9. CTA: "So if you want all these prompts and more, make sure you follow me and comment [KEYWORD] and I'll shoot them over to you." (gate = volume + bonus "and more", not withholding; FOLLOW stacked with comment)
Also in rotation: "Claude just killed [profession]" capability-tour variant (216K "Design"): claim → "let me show you how this works in the next 30 seconds" → real UI walkthrough → examples stack → price anchor ("designers charge thousands, Claude does it free") → CTA.

**VISUAL FORMULA (from frames):**
- 0-4s: talking-head hook, bright room, energetic gestures, NO cuts in first 3.5s (motion from the person, calm edit = claim-before-spectacle).
- **Static top STICKER carries a first-person RESULT-CLAIM ("Claude erased my entire digital identity 🤯") while the SPOKEN line is the QUESTION** — two different statements stacking, never duplicating (textbook compressed-headline rule).
- ~4s→end: **REAL Claude/ChatGPT UI screen recordings dominate (~80% of runtime)** — real dark Claude app, prompt typed LIVE in the composer (model picker visible), real results scrolling, real artifact cards ("Data broker removal requests · Document · MD" + Download), the connectors click-path performed for real, browser automation running. The proof IS the video.
- Talking-head returns ONLY at: the trick-flag beat (sticker swaps to "Here's the trick 👉") and the CTA (sticker swaps to "Follow and comment "X" and I'll DM you").
- **Sticker-swap system**: the persistent top sticker changes text at the 2-3 structural pivots — a mid-video re-hook lever.
- Captions: single-word ALL-CAPS white with heavy outline, one word at a time, over everything. (Alex keeps his house serif caption system per winner-lab "in-house winners outrank external for EDIT decisions" — steal the sticker-swap + real-UI dominance instead.)
