Alex (2026-07-08) flagged the core script problem: our reel scripts give away the payoff immediately, narrating the exact method so the viewer already knows what to do. When the video teaches the full how, the lead magnet is DEAD, nobody DMs for something they already got. This is the #1 script failure to avoid on every future reel. It states, at the SPOKEN-SCRIPT level, the same gate as [[reel-winning-formula]] (GATE THE PAYOFF) and [[ig-reels-scriptwriting-principles]] (GATE THE VALUE), now with concrete examples.

**Why:** the whole funnel is comment → auto-DM → guide. The ONLY reason to comment is that the actionable (the exact prompt, the setup steps, the mechanism) is missing from the video. Give it away in the VO and you get views but no comments, no DMs, no leads. Views are not the goal, comments are.

**How to apply — the script sells the RESULT + names the ARTIFACT, and withholds the HOW.**
- SAY: what it produces (the outcome they crave), that there IS a specific repeatable method, and that the exact steps/prompts live in the guide.
- DO NOT SAY: the actual mechanism, the setup steps, the "here's exactly how it works" reveal, or anything copy-pasteable.
- Replace every mechanism reveal with a CURIOSITY GAP ("there's one setup move that makes this repeatable forever, and it's in the guide").
- Pre-record self-check: "Could a viewer reproduce this from the video alone?" If yes, you gave away the how. Cut it, replace with the result + a gap.

**⛔ ANTI-EXAMPLE (what NOT to do) — the MINT script, reel 34.** It narrated the entire method in the VO:
> "Here's the part everyone misses. You do each task in front of it just once. It watches your exact clicks and keystrokes, then runs it itself, opening the tabs, filling the fields, hitting save."
That IS the complete how. The viewer now knows exactly what to do and never needs the guide, so they never comment MINT. Fix: keep "it does your busywork in the browser, click by click" (the RESULT) + "there's one setup move that makes it repeatable, it's in the guide" (the gap), and CUT the "you do it in front of it once, it watches your clicks, then runs it itself" mechanism reveal.

**✅ HOW THE VIRAL ONES GATED IT (CLONE reel 28 + BLUEPRINT reel 31, both overperformed):**
- **CLONE** ([[clone-reel-script]]): sold the RESULT ("clone Fable's brain into cheaper models so they keep working like it after the free window") and named the ARTIFACT (skill files + an instructions file). It did NOT narrate the actual audit → skills → instructions-file handoff prompts. You had to comment CLONE to get the exact prompts.
- **BLUEPRINT** ([[midnight-reel-script]]): sold the RESULT ("mass-produce build-ready specs that cheap models execute for months") and named the ARTIFACT ("the Blueprint Vault"). It did NOT give the mining-prompt or the spec template. You had to comment BLUEPRINT to get them.
- Both = OUTCOME + a named, possessable ARTIFACT + a clear GAP exactly where the copy-pasteable how should be. That gap is the thing that converts a view into a comment and a DM.

The tension to hold: [[no-anecdote-value-first-scripts]] says deliver value fast, and that still holds, but "value" in the VO = the RESULT and the desire, NOT the method. Give them proof and craving; make the mechanism the reason to comment. Pairs with [[reel-winning-formula]] and [[claude-reel-hook-library]].
