**Working YouTube transcript scrape (Claude-in-Chrome, his IG is login-walled):**
1. Get video id: on `/@nateherk/videos`, `find` the title or scrape `a[href*="/watch?v="]` titles+ids (only ~3 load without deep scroll).
2. Navigate to `watch?v=ID`. Open transcript: click `#expand` then a button whose aria-label/text matches `show transcript` (there are several; click the first).
3. Wait for the panel to LOAD (poll up to ~8s), then scrape the lightweight way: `ytd-transcript-segment-list-renderer` / `ytd-transcript-renderer` `.innerText`, split lines, drop bare `\d:\d\d` + "Transcript"/"Intro". ⚠️ NEVER scan all `div`s for innerText — it froze the renderer (CDP 45s timeout). Wrap in an IIFE (globals leak across calls → "already declared"). The `&fmt=json3` caption fetch is token-gated now (returns empty) — use the panel scrape.
