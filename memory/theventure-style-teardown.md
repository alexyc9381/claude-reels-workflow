Reverse-engineered 2026-06-16 from 3 downloaded @theventure clips (study files in `~/Downloads/theventure-study/`).

**Format:** 1080×1920, 30fps, 60–70s, single male VO, soft music bed. Same rags-to-riches arc every time:
hook → subject reveal (name + arrow) → story body w/ stat callouts → 3 lessons ("what made them successful") → valuation payoff.

**Visual layers (the signature):**
- B-roll: archival photos + founder interview footage + product/retail stock. New shot ≈ every 2s, continuous Ken-Burns zoom, soft/zoom dissolves (no hard cuts).
- Hook caption: **white** rounded box, **black** bold uppercase, multi-line, lower-third.
- Running captions: **black** rounded pill, **white** bold uppercase, 2–4 word karaoke chunks synced to VO.
- Keyword highlights: solid **yellow/green** box behind key words & $ figures.
- Name annotations: handwritten marker font (Permanent-Marker style) + hand-drawn doodle arrow pointing at the founder.
- Stat callouts: huge bold white numerals center screen.
- `@handle` watermark top-left.

Fonts used in the replica: Montserrat (display) + Permanent Marker (annotations). Implemented in [[venture-style-pipeline]].
