**⭐ THE NO-JARGON ENGINE (the #1 steal):** turn every technical thing into a **PERSONA + a plain-English OUTCOME.** A prompt isn't "an ATS-optimization prompt" → it's "**the diagnoser** — scans your resume like a real applicant tracker and tells you exactly what's broken." Name it → give it a job → state the result. Her best reel (resume, 23K likes) = 4 personas: the diagnoser / the recruiter (reads 1,000 live job posts) / the rewriter (Google's XYZ formula) / the hiring manager (interviews you, rates /10). **Contrast that recalibrates me:** ❌ "subagent orchestration with isolated context in parallel worktrees" → ✅ "turn Claude into a team of 4 specialists that split your project and tackle it at once." **REST OF THE FORMULA:**
- **Hook** = a number or curiosity gap on a sticker/ransom-note title card, mute-readable ("build a $10,000 website with 2 tools", "make your resume UNREJECTABLE", "too dangerous to release… yesterday they put it in everyone's subscription").
- **SHOW the real thing, never just describe** — always cut to the actual app/output/artifact (real resume w/ highlighted keywords, live Indeed scrolling, real Claude UI, finished website preview). **Proof > claims** ("Stripe did 2 months of eng in a day", "1,000 hrs to break it", "Meta/Google/Fortune 500").
- **One complete, DOABLE loop per reel** (diagnose→score→rewrite→prep; Claude plans→Emergent builds→live link) and **strip friction out loud** ("no code", "no developer", "about 3 minutes").
- **Friendly-friend voice** — lowercase, emojis (💌🌱👀), asides ("hope this helpsss"), a meme/reaction cutaway (dog typing, SpongeBob) for rhythm.
- **Format:** talking-head in a warm home + word-by-word full-opacity captions + screen-recording/meme inserts; ~30-65s tutorials, ~10-15s news/explainers; "diary-core scrapbook" cream/sticker aesthetic.
- **CTA:** one-word comment gate ("comment 'resume' / 'build' / 'council'") → DM the full guide/prompts. Drives huge comment counts (20-34K).
