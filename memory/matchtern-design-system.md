Official Matchtern brand system on disk: `~/Downloads/Matchtern Design System/` (`colors_and_type.css`, `SKILL.md`, `assets/logo-mark.png`). Use these EXACT tokens — do not substitute.

**Colors:** navy `#032E58` (panels/dividers/serif titles), navy-text `#001223`, stripe-blue `#5D8CC4`, **logo-blue `#2440BD`** (triangle mark + primary accent only), body-gray `#616A71`, box-fill `#B4D6EF`, box-border `#A4D4FA`, off-white `#FCFCFC`, check-green `#1F9E47`, cross-red `#D32F2F`. Palette is CLOSED: no gold/orange/purple.

**Fonts:** Playfair Display Bold = titles/hero only. Inter = everything else (cover/billboard = Inter ExtraBold).

**Logo:** the blue three-blade triangle mark = `assets/logo-mark.png`. On colored bg, place on a white rounded card (brand-approved). Never substitute a generic triangle. **The logo is the MARK ALONE — do NOT pair it with a typed "Matchtern" wordmark.** There is no official wordmark-lockup file; Alex confirmed (2026-06-16) the bare mark is the correct logo. Let the brand name appear via "matchtern.org" instead, not as hand-set text next to the mark.

**Copy rules:** no em dashes; "your child" (speaking to a parent), not "students"; "venture-backed companies" not "startups"; "matchtern.org" not ".com".
