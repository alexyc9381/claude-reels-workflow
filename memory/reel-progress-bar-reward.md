Alex wants every reel's top progress bar to END in a **reward marker** at the finish line: a checkmark / finish-line / money-back-guarantee-style seal that signals "there's a payoff if you stay to the end." It is an open-loop retention lever, not decoration.

**Why:** the progress bar is on screen the whole video; capping it with a glowing reward seal turns it into a silent promise ("stay and you get the thing"), which lifts watch-through. Pairs with the [[reel-hook-header]] (mute-readable hook) and the open-loop retention system in [[fable-reel]].

**How to apply (standing, every reel):**
- Put a distinct **reward seal at the far right end of the bar**, present + gently glowing from frame 0 (teased/locked state), so the promise is visible immediately.
- **Unlock it at the CTA** (when the payoff lands): fill it bright gold, snap in a checkmark, add a small sparkle burst + scale pop. The reward "arrives" exactly when the value/keyword does.
- Keep it visually distinct from the milestone nodes (bigger, gold, a ✓ not a number) so it reads as "prize," not "step 5."
- Mind the framing rules ([[reel-ig-feed-safezone]]): the bar sits at y~272 (below IG chrome); the seal can live at the right end there because the IG action buttons are lower (y~900+), but do not push critical glyphs past the screen edge.

**Reference implementation (FABLE reel 22, v10, `ClaudeFableReel.tsx` `ProgressBar`):** a 72px seal anchored `right:-14, top:-8`. Locked = dark fill, gold ring, dim gold ✓ (opacity 0.5), soft pulsing glow `0 0 ~12px GOLD`. Unlocked (t >= L[5], the CTA) = `grad("#F0CB63","#C98A22")` fill, light-gold border, white ✓ scaling in, 9 rising `#F3E3A6` sparkle dots, scale pop. GOLD = #E7B24C. The 4 numbered milestone nodes stay (they pop green ✓ as passed, #4 amber-teased); the reward seal is the new end-cap beyond them.
